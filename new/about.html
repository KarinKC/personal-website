About.

Every project is unique. In my designs I aim to understand the essence of the specific problem, and propose a solution with a strong concept, that drives the best user experience. My architectural background had taught me to look at every design project from an architectural point of view, applied on the digital world. I believe architecture and design share many similarities - both wish to create beautiful products, that are functional and aesthetic.
